- **Style**: Dark, cinematic, gaming-focused, high contrast
- **Color Palette**: Dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors matching the game

## Tips for Best Results

### For Midjourney:
- Add `--ar 16:9` for aspect ratio
- Add `--style raw` for more photorealistic results
- Add `--v 6` for latest version
- Use `--q 2` for higher quality

### For DALL-E 3:
- Be specific about composition and lighting
- Mention "professional gaming banner" explicitly
- Specify dark background and accent colors

### For Stable Diffusion:
- Use negative prompts: "blurry, low quality, text, watermark, amateur"
- Add "8k, high quality, professional" to positive prompts
- Use models trained on gaming/sci-fi content

### For Leonardo.ai:
- Use "Cinematic" or "Creative" preset
- Enable "Prompt Magic" for better results
- Set guidance scale to 7-10
